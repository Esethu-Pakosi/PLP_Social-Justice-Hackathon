About SolveIT Labs

SolveIT Labs transforms community challenges into actionable technological solutions. Our mission is to improve lives through innovation, collaboration, and empowerment. By listening to users and creating cutting-edge technology, we develop solutions that address socio-economic and environmental challenges.

Our Current Projects

Sentinel

Sentinel is an early warning system that uses social media data and other indicators to predict and prevent violent conflicts. It also functions as an Abduction Alert System.

MindGuard

MindGuard provides mental health resources and guidance for individuals affected by social unrest, offering care and support through digital tools.

GreenWave

GreenWave is developing tech solutions to address the waste management crisis, promoting sustainability and responsible waste disposal practices.
